About Us

Why we Choose Shaheed Mohtarma Benazir Bhutto Medical College ?

“Shaheed Mohtarma Benazir Bhutto Medical College (SMBBMC) is a renowned medical institution known for its quality education and experienced faculty. As one of the leading medical colleges, SMBBMC offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various medical specialties. With state-of-the-art facilities, well-equipped laboratories, and a commitment to research and community engagement, SMBBMC provides students with a holistic learning experience. Whether you are seeking a Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) degree or aiming for specialized training through postgraduate programs, SMBBMC stands as an excellent choice for aspiring medical professionals.”

Alumni

“SMBBMC boasts a strong network of successful alumni who have made significant contributions in the field of medicine.SMBBMC's alumni network comprises accomplished doctors, surgeons, researchers, educators, and healthcare leaders who have garnered recognition both nationally and internationally. With their extensive knowledge, skills, and experience gained from SMBBMC, these alumni have made substantial contributions to patient care, medical research, and community health. The impressive achievements of SMBBMC alumni serve as a testament to the college's commitment to producing competent and compassionate healthcare professionals.”

Self Development

“Shaheed Mohtarma Benazir Bhutto Medical College (SMBBMC) recognizes the importance of self-development in shaping well-rounded healthcare professionals. The college provides a supportive environment that encourages students to grow not only academically but also personally. SMBBMC offers various opportunities for self-development, including workshops, seminars, and extracurricular activities, aimed at enhancing skills such as communication, leadership, critical thinking, and empathy. The emphasis on self-development at SMBBMC prepares graduates to adapt to the ever-evolving healthcare landscape, deliver high-quality patient care, and become lifelong learners in their pursuit of excellence.”

MISSION

“The mission of Shaheed Mohtarma Benazir Bhutto Medical College (SMBBMC) shape its core values and aspirations. The college's mission is to provide exceptional medical education, produce competent healthcare professionals, and contribute to the improvement of community health.
