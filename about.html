MISSION

“The mission of Shaheed Mohtarma Benazir Bhutto Medical College (SMBBMC) shape its core values and aspirations. The college's mission is to provide exceptional medical education, produce competent healthcare professionals, and contribute to the improvement of community health. SMBBMC aims to nurture students with knowledge, skills, and ethical values necessary for compassionate patient care, scientific research, and community service.”

VISION

“The vision of SMBBMC is to be a leading medical institution recognized for its excellence in education, research, and healthcare delivery. The college envisions producing graduates who excel in their respective medical specialties, make significant contributions to medical advancements, and demonstrate a commitment to serving the community. SMBBMC strives to create an environment that fosters innovation, critical thinking, professionalism, and a patient-centered approach to healthcare.”
